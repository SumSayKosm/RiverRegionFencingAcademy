Who We Are

About the Club

Riverside Fencing Club is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making the sport of fencing accessible to everyone in our community. Whether you're a curious beginner or a competitive athlete, there's a place for you here.

We offer instruction in all three fencing weapons — foil, épée, and sabre — with coaches who are passionate about the sport and committed to your growth.

All equipment is provided for beginners. No experience necessary — just show up ready to learn!
